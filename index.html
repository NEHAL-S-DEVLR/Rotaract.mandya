About Us

what is rotaract ?

A Rotaract club is a Rotary-sponsored service organization for young adults (18+) focused on leadership, professional development,
and community service, combining "Rotary" and "Action"

Now what is rotaract doing in mandya ?

The Rotaract Club of Mandya, established in 2025 under Rotary Club of Mandya, is dedicated to empowering young adults through service, leadership, and fellowship.
We organize community service projects, professional development events, and social activities to foster personal growth and make a positive impact in Mandya. Our club is committed to the Rotary values of
service above self, promoting peace, and supporting education and health initiatives. Through collaboration and active engagement, we strive to create a better community and
develop future leaders.

Asvasane – Old Age Home Service

The Rotaract Club of Mandya organized Asvasane, a heartfelt service project at Seeva Kirana Old Age Home. The initiative focused on sharing food, warmth, and quality time with elderly residents, strengthening compassion and community bonding.

DROP's of hope -free vaccines for childrens

The Rotaract Club of Mandya organized DROP's of Hope this was an initiative from the indian government to provide free vaccines to children in need. The event focused on ensuring that children received essential immunizations to protect them from preventable diseases, promoting better health and well-being in the community.where 12 rotaractors volunteered to help the event run smoothly.
